Light rain

I used to be successful before I met you
I was CEO I sat on the board
I had a headcount
I was the name of a butterfly
Spoken on the radio
Breaking news

Before that I was a painter not because
I could paint but because I could see
The light that shines you through
Like a car riddled with bullet holes

And what are we
But thin skin stretched over experience
Susceptible to puncture
By the first signs of spring

I sit watching birds flit branch to branch
Like raindrops but obliquely
As if raindrops
Had another option than to fall
