Hierarchy

Long ago the sun was one of us
Blind in the garden one of us
Then one day
the sun ascended
like a prayer in the night

Our blood plasma
Drained out from the chambers of the sun
and the light from our eyes
wasn't ours anymore

God's angels were conscripted
Sorted by rank
Arranged at descending altitudes
from Heaven down
to the throne room which is exactly
where they should have remained

Now look:
See your reflection in the glass tower
See how the sunlight
projects a million angels
like street lamps watching over
the outskirts of Heaven
