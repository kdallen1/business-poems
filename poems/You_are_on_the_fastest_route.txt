You are on the fastest route

After you hang up
You will open the door
Without thinking
About flat black night or three June bugs
Who come inside without your permission
To play a dumb dirge on your wall-mount guitars

For one night only
A sky of stars will spin backwards
Now you are with your mother
Staring up at uniformed men in a post office
that never existed

One day you will hear a notification
But it was just the ding of the latch on the gate
As you closed it with your own hands
And someone you love is never coming back

Life is short and
You have to keep
Your phone charged the whole time
